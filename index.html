Strategy Guides

Getting Started in Schedule 1

First Steps

Complete the prologue to understand basic mechanics and meet key contacts.

  1. Complete Prologue
    • Learn movement and interaction
    • Meet Dan at the hardware store
    • Understand basic buying/selling
  2. First Operations
    • Start with cannabis for low risk
    • Purchase grow tent from Dan's Hardware
    • Order seeds from Albert via phone
  3. Recruit Your First Dealer
    • Find Benji through the tutorial
    • Assign territory and products
    • Monitor sales and adjust pricing
  4. Scale Your Operations
    • Buy first property through Rays Realty
    • Set up efficient grow operation
    • Hire employees from Warehouse District

Advanced Strategies

Optimal Production Layout

Use the 8-tent configuration for maximum efficiency:

  • 2 rows of 4 tents facing each other
  • Central supply shelf for easy access
  • Dedicated botanist for automation
  • Large shelf for processed product storage

Economic Optimization

  • Match product quality to district wealth
  • Automate supply chains through employees
  • Diversify product lines to capture all markets
  • Invest in multiple properties for redundancy

Law Enforcement Management

  • Hide illegal items when police presence increases
  • Use multiple safe houses for storage
  • Maintain legitimate businesses as fronts
  • Build political connections for protection
